Who I Am

About Me

🏆
Innovation & Technology Award — NESR Recognised on stage for outstanding contribution to innovation and technology in the energy sector

I am a Senior Operations and Quality Leader with over two decades of hands-on experience in the Oil & Gas industry. My career spans well testing, ISO/API Q2 quality systems, produced water treatment, and the deployment of cutting-edge ESG technologies.

Currently serving as Global Operations Manager at NESR (National Energy Services Reunited) in Al Khobar, Saudi Arabia, I lead the global delivery of ESG and decarbonization solutions — including Direct Lithium Extraction, GHG detection, and Zero Liquid Discharge water projects.

From frontline field operations in Pakistan to regional training roles across East Asia and quality leadership in Saudi Arabia, I have built a career defined by operational excellence, quality governance, and technological innovation.
